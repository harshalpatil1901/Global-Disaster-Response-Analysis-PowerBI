Global Disaster Response Analysis (2018–2024)
Project Overview:

The Global Disaster Response Analysis (2018–2024) is a data-driven Power BI dashboard developed to evaluate disaster trends, impact severity, and emergency response performance across multiple regions worldwide.

This project analyzes historical disaster data to identify patterns in disaster occurrence, assess human and economic impact, and measure operational efficiency in response management. The objective is to transform complex datasets into structured, interactive insights that support strategic planning and informed decision-making.

Business Problem:

Organizations involved in disaster management often lack a centralized analytical system to monitor disaster frequency, assess response effectiveness, and evaluate resource allocation.

Without an integrated dashboard, stakeholders face challenges in:

Identifying high-risk regions

Measuring response efficiency

Evaluating economic and human impact

Monitoring long-term disaster trends

Optimizing aid distribution strategies

Objectives:

Analyze disaster frequency by year and region

Measure total casualties and economic losses

Evaluate response time and recovery duration

Assess response efficiency using performance indicators

Identify deadliest and costliest disaster types

Support data-driven disaster preparedness strategies

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Total Number of Disasters

Total Countries Affected

Total Casualties

Total Economic Loss

Average Response Time

Total Aid Distributed

Average Recovery Duration

Average Response Efficiency Score
